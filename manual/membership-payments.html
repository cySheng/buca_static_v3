Membership payments

Whether it's the monthly security fee or an annual membership due, BUCA keeps every payment in one place — so you always know who's paid, who hasn't, and where the money came from.

How payments work in BUCA

A payment in BUCA is simply a record that a household paid an amount, on a date, for a reason. There are two ways they get created:

  • By the committee — you record a payment when someone pays you cash, bank transfer or cheque.
  • By residents — residents can record their own payment against their household, which you then confirm.

Either way, the payment lands in the same place and counts towards that household's status.

Security fee or membership due?

BUCA treats them the same way — you just label each payment with what it's for. That keeps your collection for the security fund separate from annual dues when you export.

Recording a payment

  1. Find the household

    Go to Payments → Record payment and search for the member or house number.

  2. Enter the details

    Fill in the amount, the date paid, and what it's for (for example, Security fee — June 2026). Add a note if there's anything to remember.

  3. Save

    The payment is recorded instantly and the household's status updates to Paid for that period.

Seeing who's paid and who's due

The Payments screen shows your whole association for the period you choose. Each household is marked Paid, Due or Partial, and the collection rate sits right at the top. Filter to just the Due list when it's time to send reminders.

Letting residents record their own

To save the committee work, residents can log their own payment from their account — handy after a bank transfer. It appears in your Pending list for you to confirm, so you stay in control of what counts.

Fewer "did you get my payment?" messages

When residents can see their own status and record transfers, the committee's WhatsApp goes a lot quieter.

Exporting your payments

Need the numbers for a committee meeting, the treasurer's report or ROS? Press Export on the Payments screen and BUCA gives you the full list as a spreadsheet — every payment, who paid, when, and what for — with one click.

You can also store invoices and generate printable payment vouchers to keep your books audit-ready. Read the invoices & vouchers guide for that.
